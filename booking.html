Booking Policy

Our booking policy ensures fair, transparent, and hassle-free rental of agricultural machinery. Please read the terms carefully before reserving any equipment.

1. Booking Confirmation

Bookings must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Confirmation is given after approval from our team.

2. Rental Charges

Charges are based on hourly, daily, or acreage usage. Customized rates available.

3. Equipment Availability

Equipment is allotted on a first-come, first-served basis.

4. Cancellation Policy

Cancel at least 12 hours before the booking time to avoid charges.

5. Damage & Responsibility

Damages due to careless handling will be charged to the renter.

6. Operator Requirement

Some machines require a trained operator, available at extra cost.

7. Identification

Valid ID is required during equipment handover.

8. Timely Return

Late return may incur additional charges.

9. Payment Terms

Payment accepted in cash or online. Taxes may apply.
